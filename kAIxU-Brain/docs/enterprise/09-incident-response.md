# 09. Incident Response Workflows

## Severity Model

- **Sev1:** full outage / major customer impact.
- **Sev2:** critical feature broken (smoke fail sustained).
- **Sev3:** degraded performance or partial impact.
- **Sev4:** low-risk defect.

## Lifecycle

1. Detect and declare incident.
2. Assign commander + comms lead.
3. Mitigate and stabilize.
4. Recover and monitor.
5. Publish postmortem in 5 business days.
