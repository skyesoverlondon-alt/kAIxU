# Data Lifecycle Policy

## Data Classes

- Public
- Internal
- Confidential
- Restricted

## Retention

- Operational telemetry: 90 days default.
- Audit evidence: 365 days minimum.
- Incident records: 365+ days per policy.

## Deletion

- Scheduled deletion job for expired records.
- Right-to-delete requests tracked with ticket + evidence.

## Access Governance

- Least privilege role-based access.
- Quarterly access review for privileged roles.
